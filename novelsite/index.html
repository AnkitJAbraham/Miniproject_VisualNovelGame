Introduction


Visual narrative games are video games that focus on storytelling through visuals, using cutscenes, dialogue, and character interactions to advance the narrative. They are based on the visual novel genre of literature. A notable example is "Ace Attorney", a Japanese visual novel based game. Interactive storytelling is a form of digital entertainment in which the storyline is not predetermined. The author creates the setting, characters, and situation which the narrative must address, but the user (also reader or player) experiences a unique story based on their interactions and choices.

Therefore, our goal was to produce a playable game which combines the above characteristics.

Presenting ....

The Merchant Of Venice.


Project Objective


  • Develop an interactive gameplay system with puzzles and challenges, seamlessly integrated into the narrative experience.
  • Implement branching storylines with weighted choices, ensuring player decisions have meaningful consequences on the narrative progression.
  • Optimize game performance and loading times through efficient coding practices and resource management within the Ren'Py engine.
  • Create a visually appealing and immersive experience through the integration of sound effects, music, and visual elements that enhance the atmosphere of the game.

Purpose and Need


Convey important moral messages within the game, focusing on themes such as prejudice, love, friendship, and justice. Provide guidance and offer insights into the consequences of one's actions, encouraging players to make thoughtful choices throughout the game. Showcase the rich setting of Renaissance Venice, with its vibrant markets, opulent palaces, and intriguing characters, to provide a visually stunning and immersive gameplay experience.


By combining the timeless story of "The Merchant of Venice" with the interactive and immersive nature of a visual novel game, this project seeks to engage players of all ages, fostering a deeper appreciation for Shakespeare's work and offering a fresh perspective on the moral dilemmas explored in the play.

Features


  • Implement branching storylines with weighted choices, ensuring player decisions have meaningful consequences on the narrative progression.
  • Multiple decision points throughout the story, with many of them carrying weightage (consequences), and others with differing paths each time.
  • The result of the game's climax would be the determination of all the weighted values, paths and decisions taken, with multiple story endings.
